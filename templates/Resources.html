Precautions

  • Avoid harsh soaps and detergents.
  • Moisturize your skin regularly.
  • Protect your skin from excessive sun exposure.
  • Identify and avoid allergens.
  • Consult a dermatologist for persistent skin issues.

Recommended Foods

  • Foods rich in Omega-3 fatty acids (fish, flaxseeds).
  • Fruits and vegetables high in antioxidants (berries, spinach).
  • Foods with Vitamin D (fortified milk, eggs).
  • Hydrating foods (cucumber, watermelon).
  • Foods with zinc (nuts, seeds).
