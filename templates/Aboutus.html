Our Mission

At DermDetect, we are committed to making skin disease detection accessible, accurate, and efficient. Our web-based platform combines medical expertise with cutting-edge artificial intelligence to help users identify potential skin conditions and connect them with appropriate healthcare resources.

Who We Are

Founded in 2024, DermDetect was born from a collaboration between dermatologists, computer scientists, and public health advocates who recognized the need for better access to skin disease screening tools. Our diverse team brings together expertise in medical imaging, machine learning, dermatological diagnostics, and user experience design to create a platform that serves healthcare professionals and patients alike.

Our Technology

Our skin disease detection system utilizes a convolutional neural network trained on over 500,000 clinically verified images of various skin conditions. The technology can identify more than 200 different skin diseases with accuracy comparable to board-certified dermatologists. Our platform is continuously updated with the latest research findings and undergoes regular validation through clinical testing.

Our Commitment to Privacy

We understand the sensitive nature of medical data. All images uploaded to our platform are encrypted, anonymized, and handled in full compliance with HIPAA regulations. We do not sell user data to third parties, and users maintain complete control over their information at all times.

Our Vision for the Future

We envision a world where preventable skin diseases are detected early, regardless of geography or socioeconomic status. We are actively working to expand our platform's capabilities to include more rare conditions and to make our technology available in low-resource settings through mobile solutions and partnerships with global health organizations.
